COVID-19 Risk Assessment Tool

A set of solutions designed to help quickly identify coronavirus symptoms and get reliable information regarding COVID-19 concerns.

How it helps people
Triage
The COVID-19 health risk is clearly
stated at the end of the
thanks to the five-level triage.
Recommendation
Our Medical Team has prepared
detailed recommendations and
advice on what to do next
depending on the interview results.
Local information
We provide phone numbers of local
health authorities so users know
who to contact in an emergency.
How to use it
We’ve created this tool for the benefit of both communities and individuals.
We believe we can prevent panic and relieve health systems by providing
quick access to credible and medically correct information. No matter how
you decide to use the tool, you can help many. Act now.
Embed anywhere
If you are a clinic, health organization, journalist
or NGO, feel free to embed the COVID-19 Risk
Assessment tool on your website. Let's stop the
coronavirus from spreading.
Use API
Support the global fight against the coronavirus
outbreak with your own solution. Use the
COVID-19 Risk Assessment API in your own way.
It is available for free, forever, so you can do your
best to stop the spread of the virus.
Try and share
We have extended the functionality of Symptomate, our
symptom checking app, to assist patients with
coronavirus-related health concerns. It can provide you
with up-to-date information and recommendations.
Why you can trust this tool
Based on reliable sources
We want our tool to be safe and reliable,
so its logic is based on the official global
information provided by the WHO.
Verified by doctors
The content was meticulously
implemented by Infermedica’s team
of doctors. It is kept up to date with
the latest WHO and CDC guidelines.
